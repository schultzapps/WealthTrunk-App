FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is WealthTrunk?

WealthTrunk is a native Apple personal finance experience designed to help you track your net worth, manage multiple profiles, and plan for your financial future. Whether you're tracking individual finances or managing wealth as a family, WealthTrunk gives you a clear picture of where you stand today and where you're headed.

How is my data stored and protected?

Your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. Your information is encrypted and only you can access your data—we cannot see, read, or access any of your financial information.

What can I do for free?

Free users can track 6 accounts, create profiles for family members, view net worth charts and projections, record historical balances, and export PDF statements. Pro removes the account limit and adds customization features.

Can I share my subscription with family?

Yes! If you subscribe and have Apple Family Sharing enabled, up to 5 family members can access WealthTrunk Pro at no additional cost. If you want to share data with a partner, use the backup feature to clone your data to their device. A family syncing feature is on the roadmap.

Will my data sync across devices?

Yes! When you're signed into iCloud, your data automatically syncs across all your Apple devices—iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Any changes you make on one device appear on your other devices within moments.

Can I sync with my bank automatically?

WealthTrunk uses manual entry by design—no bank connections means no data risk, no disconnections leaving gaps in your history, and no security concerns. Taking a few minutes to update your balances creates intentionality around your finances that often leads to better results.

How often should I log my balances?

This is personal preference and the app is designed to be flexible. Some users log balances weekly or monthly, while others do it quarterly or annually. The key is consistency—find a rhythm that works for you and stick with it.
